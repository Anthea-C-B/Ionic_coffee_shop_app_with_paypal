About Coffee:

Coffee is brewed drink prepared from roasted coffee beans, which are the seeds of berries from the Coffea plant. The genus Coffea is native to tropical Africa (specifically having its origin in Ethipia and Sudan) and Madagascar, the Comoros, Mauritius, and Reunion in the Indian Ocean. The plant was exportedt from Africa to countries around the world and coffee plants are now cultivated in over 70 countires, primarily in the equatorial regions of the Americas, Southeast Asia, India, and Africa. The two most commonly grown are the highly regarded arabica, and the less sophisticated but stronger and more hardy robusta. Once ripe, coffee berries are picked, processed, and dried. Dried coffee seeds (referred to as beans) are roasted to varying degrees, depending on the desired flavor. Roasted beans are gorund and brewed with near-boiling water to produce as a beverage.
